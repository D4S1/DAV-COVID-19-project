COVID-19 Analysis Report - Australia

Executive Summary

This report provides an overview of the impact and progression of the COVID-19 pandemic in Australia, analyzing key trends and data-driven insights. The findings are based on publicly available datasets and visualized to highlight patterns over time. This project was developed as part of the Data Analysis and Visualization course at the University of Warsaw. All values in tables are current as of May 24 2025.
